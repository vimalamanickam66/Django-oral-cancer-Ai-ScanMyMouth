How OralGuard AI Works
Our pipeline combines computer vision preprocessing with a trained CNN and explainable AI visualization to deliver accurate, interpretable results.
01
📸
Image Upload or Capture
Upload a file or take a photo directly. The quality checker ensures the image is clear enough for accurate analysis.
02
⚙️
Preprocessing
OpenCV resizes and normalizes the image to 224×224 pixels with centre crop for consistent model input.
03
🧬
CNN Inference
MobileNetV2 transfer-learning model classifies tissue as cancerous or normal with probability scores.
04
🔥
Grad-CAM Heatmap
Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping highlights the regions that influenced the prediction.
05
📋
Clinical Report
A structured report delivers severity level, risk factors, lesion analysis, and actionable recommendations.
06
💾
Record Stored
Every analysis is persisted with full metadata for historical review and clinical audit trails.
